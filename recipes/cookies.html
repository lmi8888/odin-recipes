Sugar Cookies

Description

Recipe from Beyond Frosting

This Easy Sugar Cookie Recipe makes homemade cookies that are buttery, dense and chewy in the middle. Top these sugar cookies with a vanilla frosting, or roll them sprinkles. Drop cookies are perfect when you don't feel like going through all the hassle to make cut out cookies!

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Large eggs
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour & Baking soda
  • Cream of tartar

Steps

  1. Allow the butter to soften for 30 minutes or soften the butter in the microwave for 10-12 seconds.
  2. Combine the butter with the sugar and beat on medium speed until well creamed together.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract and until well beaten
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the remaining dry ingredients: flour, baking soda and cream of tartar. Slowly add into batter and beat on medium low speed until well combined and dough forms.
  5. Use a large cookie scoop and scoop cookie dough onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350° F. Place the dough about 2 inches apart, and let the dough sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes prior to baking.
  7. Bake at 350° F for 10-12 minutes. Allow cookie to cool on the baking sheet for several minutes and then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely
